## RECORDING TIPS

- **Pace**: Slightly slower than conversational. ~150 words/minute.
- **Mouse**: Move deliberately. No jittery cursor. Hover on elements you're discussing.
- **Pauses**: 1-second pause between scenes. Let visuals register.
- **Audio**: Record voiceover separately if possible (cleaner). Or narrate live.
- **Length**: Script is ~280 words at 150 wpm = ~1:52. You WILL need to trim. Cut pauses, not content. Target exactly 90 seconds.
- **If over 90s**: Cut Scene 5 second paragraph (Move contract) and shorten Scene 4.
